VIT OUTDOOR STADIUM

The VIT Outdoor Stadium is a well-maintained sports facility that offers ample space for various outdoor games and athletic activities. Equipped with a standard pitch, multiple goalposts, and open grounds, it serves as a hub for students to engage in football, cricket, athletics, and other sports. Surrounded by a lively campus atmosphere, the stadium not only promotes physical fitness but also fosters teamwork and sportsmanship among students.
